Unique Chess

A working custom chess game using your board background and unique handmade piece assets.

Choose Play vs Computer or Play vs Person. In two-player mode, the board flips after every move so the current player is on the bottom. Use standard chess rules with your custom pieces. Checkmate the Cross King to win.

Medium is smarter. Hard is really hard and looks several moves ahead.

Undo rules: Easy has unlimited undos. Medium has 15 undos. Hard has 3 undos.

Play vs Person: There are no undos in two-player mode.
